CHARACTER GUIDES

Master your agents with our comprehensive character guides and team building strategies.

ANBY

Anby is a skilled agent with lightning-fast reflexes and powerful electric abilities. Her unique combat style makes her a versatile addition to any team.

ABILITIES

  • Lightning Strike: Deals area damage to enemies
  • Thunder Flash: Quick dash that damages enemies in path
  • Storm Surge: Ultimate ability that creates a powerful electric field

RECOMMENDED BUILD

Focus on attack speed and electric damage bonuses for maximum effectiveness.

BILLY

Billy is a heavy-hitting tank with impressive defensive capabilities. His ability to draw enemy attention makes him an excellent frontline fighter.

ABILITIES

  • Ground Slam: Creates shockwaves that stun enemies
  • Iron Wall: Temporary defensive buff for the team
  • Mountain Crush: Ultimate ability that deals massive damage in a cone

RECOMMENDED BUILD

Prioritize defense stats and cooldown reduction for consistent ability usage.

NICOLE

Nicole is a support specialist with healing abilities and team buffs. Her strategic value makes her essential for challenging content.

ABILITIES

  • Healing Wave: Restores health to all team members
  • Protection Field: Creates a shield that absorbs damage
  • Divine Blessing: Ultimate ability that provides powerful team buffs

RECOMMENDED BUILD

Focus on healing effectiveness and energy regeneration for sustained support.
